To pack a release for Nexus, pack MusicMakerRandomizer into a .zip and upload it to Nexus, nothing else is needed.

To pack a release for Thunderstore, create a .zip like this:
- plugins (renamed from MusicMakerRandomizer)
- manifest.json
- icon.png
- README.md
- CHANGELOG.md
- LICENSE

Name the .zip file "NikkelM-Randomize_Music_Maker_Song_Choice.zip" and upload it to Thunderstore.
